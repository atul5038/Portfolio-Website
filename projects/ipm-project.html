IPM Monitoring for Fennel & Coriander (ITC)

Project Overview

Worked with farmers cultivating fennel and coriander crops to monitor Integrated Pest Management (IPM) compliance and improve crop quality.

Key Responsibilities

  • Visited multiple farmers and monitored fennel and coriander crops at different growth stages.
  • Collected crop samples and coordinated laboratory testing.
  • Verified IPM compliance and quality standards.
  • Compared farmers and regions based on compliance performance.
  • Identified areas with the highest number of IPM-compliant farmers.

Skills Used

  • Agriculture Operations
  • Field Monitoring
  • Data Collection
  • Quality Control
  • Farmer Coordination
  • Reporting & Analysis
